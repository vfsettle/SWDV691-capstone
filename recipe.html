Buffalo Cauliflower Bites

A spicy, healthy, vegetarian, delicious alternative to chicken wings, these cauliflower buffalo wings have been pinned over 600,000 times because they are so good!


Ingredients


6 SERVINGS:
1 head cauliflower
1 tablespoon olive oil

BATTER
1/2 cup flow
1 teaspoon whole milk
1 teaspoon hot sauce

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 450˚F (230˚C).
  2. Cut the head of cauliflower in half. Pluck bite-sized florets from each half. Trim bottoms of florets as necessary.
  3. In a medium bowl, mix the batter ingredients.
  4. Add cauliflower to the batter. Stir so each floret is fully coated.
  5. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Spread cauliflower florets on the baking sheet and drizzle with olive oil.
  6. Bake for 20 minutes.
  7. Remove the cauliflower from the oven and toss with buffalo sauce.
  8. Return cauliflower to oven and bake for an additional 10 minutes until browned to your liking.
  9. Enjoy!

Tips

vicky vegan

These were amazing! I used buffalo sauce instead of hot sauce in the batter and I used garlic powder instead of garlic salt. I also used unsweetened almond milk, it tasted great and it’s a great vegan alternative. Highly recommend making, super easy and great for any game day.

mama mazing

I beat one egg with about 1 tbsp of the buffalo sauce in it. Then I rolled the cauliflower in seasoned flour, next I dipped the cauliflower into the egg, then once more into the flour (no milk) I still baked them the exact same way and they turned out so crispy on the outside and moist on the inside! Then I tossed them in the buffalo sauce! Amazing!

manic mondays

Don't try to use foil in place of parchment paper. I recommend using olive oil spray to save your baking pan. For a dipping, we love 50/50 blue cheese dressing with Frank’s Hot Wing Buffalo sauce!
